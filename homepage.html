Latest News

Vans has just announced the debut of its "Eco Classics" line, a green shoe range made up of recycled content and bio-based soles. The range revamps the brand's most iconic shapes like the Old Skool and Sk8-Hi using eco-friendly production methods. In addition, Vans is offering "25% off for new customers" on its web store and in some of its retail stores this month as part of initiatives to attract sustainable Gen Z consumers. This is the latest move following the brand's broader initiatives towards sustainability and innovation in street wear.

Vans Skateboarding Hosts Thrilling International Events in 2025- Vans Skateboarding continues to lead the pack with its jaw-dropping 2025 event schedule, recently producing sold-out events in Los Angeles, Paris, and Tokyo. The Vans Park Series and iconic House of Vans pop-ups drew premier skaters and fans alike, with new faces, tricks, and lots of camaraderie. Emphasizing progression and creativity, Vans reasserted its position as a worldwide leader in skate culture and pledged even more high-energy contests throughout the year.

Vans BMX Ignites 2025 Season with High-Flying Action - Vans BMX Kicks Off 2025 Season with Sky-Pumping Action Vans BMX kicked off its season for 2025 with pulse-thumping events that brought top-caliber riders onto the planet. The Vans BMX Pro Cup series opened in fireworks-like style in Sydney, awe-inspiring moves and stiff competition for spectators to witness before a packed crowd. With other stops in Europe and South America, the tour continues to push boundaries in freestyle BMX and revel in the raw energy and innovation of the sport. Get ready for even more gravity-defying action as the heat picks up.

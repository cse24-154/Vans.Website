Our Story- Since 1966, Vans has been more than just a shoe company—it's a symbol of self-expression, creativity, and the spirit of youth culture. Born in Southern California and built from the ground up by skaters, surfers, artists, and dreamers, Vans quickly became a movement. What started as a small shop selling custom canvas shoes has evolved into a global icon of streetwear and action sports. Our signature styles like the Old Skool, Sk8-Hi, and Authentic aren’t just footwear—they’re canvases for individuality. At Vans, we believe in doing things our own way. Whether it's supporting local skate scenes, collaborating with bold artists, or pushing boundaries in design, we're always about staying true to who we are. This is our story—and it’s still being written by you.
